Hourly Coverage
How to read this
Pool active = users from your fixed pool who are active that hour, distributed by random start times across the window.

Supply can serve = max users the hour's order volume can absorb (from the main formula).

Dead time = active users who cannot be matched to an order that hour — the supply is exhausted by others. They are online but idle.

Increase the pool size to see more dead time. Decrease it to see under-coverage (orders go unfulfilled).
